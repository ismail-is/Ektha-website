About Us

Covenant for Community Advancement

Humanitarian Commitment

Established as a charitable non-profit entity, the Indian Association of Cote D'Ivoire is committed to spearheading humanitarian causes, primarily focusing on health and education within the local community. With an unwavering dedication, the association strives to uplift the social, educational, economic, and cultural welfare of the people and residents of Cote D'Ivoire, fostering a sense of unity and solidarity.


Cross-Cultural Bridging

An essential facet of the association's mission is to facilitate cultural, economic, and social exchanges between the people of India and the diverse communities residing in Cote D'Ivoire. By supporting and nurturing relationships, the association aims to build bridges and establish branches across the Republic of Cote D'Ivoire. This expansive network enhances efficient administration and ensures the seamless realization of its diverse objectives.


Empowerment and Collaborative Progress

Beyond its charitable pursuits, the association actively engages in empowering communities by establishing institutions, promoting youth wings, and coordinating various community activities. Through fundraising, managing trust funds, and strategic investments, the association envisions contributing to the educational, healthcare, and social welfare sectors. These concerted efforts reflect the association's commitment to fostering harmony, mutual understanding, and sustainable progress within the vibrant landscape of the Republic of Cote D'Ivoire.


Mission & Vision

Indian Association of Cote D'lvoire is a non-profit, secular, civic, charitable and cultural organization established in 2014 to serve the Indian community in Cote D'lvoire.Our mission is to provide opportunities and a positive environment to the community to fulfill its needs and aspirations for social and cultural interaction, and to help raise understanding and awareness of Indian traditions and cultural heritage particularly among the younger generation.EKTHA-CI also promotes understanding and assimilation with African society and organizations by encouraging a spirit of volunteerism among its members.

Cultural Events

Indian Association of Cote D'lvoire represents Indian community at large and organizes various socio-cultural activities/events throughout the year with the active participation of members. It is the face of Indian community and participates in all major festivals and celebrations in and around the city.Our membership is open all year round for the benefit of new and prospective members. We support various worthy causes at local, national, and international levels by physical (volunteering) and financial support through donations.
